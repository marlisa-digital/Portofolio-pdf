My story

How I got here

"I didn't plan to end up in marketing. But once I saw how the same thinking applied — I couldn't unsee it."

For almost 9 years, I worked in healthcare insurance. My job was to analyse medical claims — reading doctor's notes, checking whether the diagnosis matched the treatment, whether the medication aligned with the complaint, whether the lab results supported everything else. Every element had to connect. If one piece didn't add up, the claim wouldn't go through.

Turns out, that's exactly how I look at a business funnel now.

When sales aren't coming in, most people blame the ads. But the real question is: where does the logic break down? Is the website confusing visitors the moment they land? Does the product messaging make people curious but not convinced enough to buy? Is the brand speaking in a way that creates doubt instead of trust? Just like a medical claim — everything has to connect. Traffic, messaging, website experience, offer clarity. One broken link and the conversion doesn't go through.

That's what I look for. And once you see it, you can't unsee it.

FAQ

Questions you might be wondering

Honest answers before you decide to work together.

How long does an audit take?

For Social Media Audits and Website/Funnel Audits, typically 3–5 business days from when all access and briefs are received. For more complex dashboards or strategies, it can be 7–14 days. I always confirm the timeline upfront before we start.

What tool access do you need?

It depends on the service. For website/funnel audits, I need read-only GA4 access and optionally Google Search Console. For social media audits, access to your account's Insights/Analytics is sufficient. I never ask for full admin access — read-only is enough for analysis.

Is this suitable for a brand-new business?

Absolutely. New businesses actually benefit most from an early audit — before spending budget on the wrong strategy. I have experience working with businesses at different stages, from brand-new launches all the way to established operations.

Is there a results guarantee?

I can't guarantee specific conversion numbers since many factors are outside my control (product, pricing, market). What I do guarantee: an honest, specific, and immediately actionable audit report — not generic advice. If you're not satisfied, I'll revise until the output is truly useful.
